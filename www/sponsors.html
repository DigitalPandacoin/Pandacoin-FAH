Sponsorship Opportunities

We provide a system for sponsorship allowing flexible weekly contributions.
A sponsor will be given a personal hot-wallet to fund their contributions.
An administrator will work with a sponsor to configure a scheduled automatic weekly contribution from this wallet.
This can be accompanied by a plaque of recognition or simple advertisement on this website.
The sponsorship system can be configured to automatically contribute a specific value weekly, a percentage to match existing contributions/donations, or just the contents of your wallet each week.
An advertisement plaque can show a title, small bit of text, small image, and link to a web URL.
All sponsors ads are left to the discretion of the administrators and subject to approval.
